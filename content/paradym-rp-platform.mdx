## Paradym for Relying Parties

The Paradym wallet is part of the [Paradym platform](https://paradym.id/). A commercial solution for Relying Parties to easily integrate with all member state wallets.

<img src="/images/overview.png" alt="Platform overview" className="max-w-md mx-auto" />

Parties who use Paradym, either through its on-premise or cloud capacity, can:

- Set issuance templates
- Set verification templates
- Integrate and remain interoperable with all National and other certified wallets.
- Monitor activity of triggered issuances and verifications
- Add and manage trusted lists
- Register parties in National trusted lists
- Handle ETSI compliance
